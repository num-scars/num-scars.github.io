Venue

The workshop will take place at CWI Amsterdam, and the Amsterdam science park congress center right next door. For (public transport) directions to CWI click here . In all public transportation, you can check in and out with a Mastercard or Visa contactless credit card and also with Apple Pay and Google Wallet.
